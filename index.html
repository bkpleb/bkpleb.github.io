Agriculture & food systems

I've always been drawn to the land. For a little over two months, before heading off to uni, I was studying agriculture at Northern Lodge, learning as much as I could on cropping, cattle management and ag machinery.

Traditional agriculture is on its way to having a slow and painful death. With environmental changes/degradation, increasing water scarcity and fuel/input costs, it's becoming unsustainable. Though the breakthrough of autonomous agricultural robotics and AI is exciting, the way we farm is still fundamentally unchanged.

Passionate about:

  • hyper-efficient production of horticultural crops with minimal resources through vertical farming, agri-biotech and precision fermentation to ensure Australian food security.
